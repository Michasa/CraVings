FED by Water //

Dalston, East London

Italian food – who could live without it? The Vegan Italian presence has never been stronger in London and FED by Water continues to be a stand-out choice.

Read More/Less...

Forget having to choose between one or two vegan pizzas; FED has an incredible offering of delicious pizzas and calzones for you to choose from, some including quirky toppings like activated charcoal and pear.

If you’re more of a pasta person, they do a killer carbonara and a lasagne. In true Italian fashion, FED do not skimp on the portion sizes.

It is also worth mentioning that FED has incredible tap water (yes, you read right), which is arguably as popular as their food. Using special carbon filtration technologies, FED rid their tap water of impurities, creating an undeniably magnificent glass of water.

Get down to Dalston and let us know what you think - Buon Appetito!
