Star Eats of the Week

As chosen by Seraphina
Itadaki Zen //

Kings Cross, North London

Stashed changes Europes first organic and vegan Japanese restaurant, Itadaki Zen is definitely a great place to check out. The bento boxes are delicious and adorable, the udon will tantalise your tastebuds and the sushi is super!

Read More/Less...

Embracing the Far Eastern tradition of appreciating the medicinal value of plants and food Itadaki Zen seeks to provide wholesome, healing, tasty food.

Itadaki means ‘to take the food life’; Zen means ‘to fix, to mend’. The atmosphere and cusine will keep you coming back time and again.

Friendly and inviting, with free workshops and artistic events happening regularly as well as healthy delicious food, this place will keep you coming back for more.

Itadakimasu mina-san! ♡
