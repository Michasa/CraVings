About CraVe

Cravings is your one-stop destination for all of your plant-based desires. Whether you are vegan, 'v-curious', or a 'reductarian', we're here to make sure you never have to compromise your cravings.

By providing you with up-to-date information about the locations of Vegan restaurants, cafes and pubs in London, we hope to make existing and transitioning Vegan’s journeys that bit easier.

We also want to show those wishing to incorporate more plant-based food into their diets that they do not need to compromise on variety or flavour. There is so much plant-based goodness out there and CraVe is here to help you track it down.

Hannah

Hello World! I’m Hannah – a 20 year old student from Bath University.

I have been a Pescatarian since the age of six. In 2017, I tried to transition to a Veganism, a.k.a my ‘Seagan’ phase.

After reading Fat Gay Vegan’s Fat Gay Vegan: Eat, Drink and Live Like You Give a Sh!t, I decided to dive headfirst into fully fledged Veganism. After a rocky start (I may have broken down and had a packet of mini cheddars), I have now been plant-based for 2 months.

Despite living in London, one of the most Vegan-friendly cities in the world, I often feel like my only snacking options are a piece of fruit or a falafel wrap. So, this website was partially inspired by my inability to shut up about how much I miss the 3 big c’s – cake, chocolate and cheese.

I hope to continue developing the features of this website with the arsenal of skills I’ve learnt over the past 8 weeks.
